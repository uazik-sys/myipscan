How accurate is IP geolocation?

2025-10-11 · by myipscan@gmail.com

IP geolocation is approximate. City-level accuracy is common, but it depends on your provider and network.

What it can show

  • Country and region
  • Nearest major city
  • Your provider/ASN

What it cannot show

  • Your exact street address
  • Your personal name
