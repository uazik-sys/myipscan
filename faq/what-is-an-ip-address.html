What is an IP address?

2025-10-11 · by myipscan@gmail.com

An IP address is the public number of your internet connection. Websites use it to send responses back to you.

Two types: IPv4 and IPv6

IPv4 uses 32-bit addresses like 203.0.113.7. IPv6 uses 128-bit addresses like 2001:db8::1. Many networks support both.

Does an IP show my name?

No. An IP shows your approximate region and provider (ASN), not your personal identity.
